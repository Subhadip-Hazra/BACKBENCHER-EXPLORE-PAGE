Welcome to Asansol Engineering College

Explore a world of knowledge.

Asansol Engineering College, situated in Asansol, West Bengal, India, offers a range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs in Engineering, Technology, and related fields. Established in 1998 through a collaboration between the JIS Group and the Techno India Group, the institution is affiliated with Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology and holds approval from the All India Council for Technical Education. Supported by a World Bank grant under the TEQIP initiative, the college's campus is situated on Vivekananda Sarani in Kanyapur, Asansol.
